Signal quality — what's actually IMSA-relevant

Of 1,654 raw rows, only 31.3% come from videos that are actually about racing. The rest are off-topic videos where commenters happened to mention an IMSA-tracked keyword. Honest pitch: this is the filtering layer that turns harvested signal into sponsorship-quality audience data.
